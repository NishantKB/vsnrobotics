What is VSN Robotics?

VSN Robotics Club is a club managed by V.S. Education Foundation that focuses on robots and technology. It started in 2021 when COVID-19 was a big problem. Despite this, the club has done well in many robot competitions, like science shows, robot races, and robot soccer. We also organize these events for others. We aim to inspire young people and help them learn about robots and technology.

Empowering Young Minds

Empowering Young Minds, nurturing the potential of young enthusiasts is our mission. We conduct workshops and classes, including drone and robo race sessions, at school and high school levels, fostering a passion for robotics and technology. Since 2021, we have excelled in various competitions, including science exhibitions, robo races, and robo soccer, while also hosting these events.
